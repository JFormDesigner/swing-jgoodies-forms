What are the main benefits of the Forms?

Forms aims to make the layout process faster, better and easier. It makes simple things easy, and the hard stuff possible, good design easy and the bad difficult.

Can I use the Forms in a commercial product?

You can use and redistribute the Forms in source and binary form - even for commercial use - if you comply with the terms of the BSD open source license.

Where do I get the latest version?

The latest version is available in the project's file sharing section, where you can download the Forms library, sources, tutorial, documentation and a whitepaper.

Where do I get support?

Forms is unofficially supported at JavaDesktop.org, see http://forms.dev.java.net. In case of a problem, please check out the archive of the user mailing list first. If you can't find anything useful, you may mail to users@forms.dev.java.net.

Priority support is available for customers of the JGoodies Looks Pro and JGoodies Swing Suite products. In addition to the support at JavaDesktop.org, customers can send mail to the JGoodies technical mailing list or directly to support@JGoodies.com.

How can I help improve the Forms?

I'd appreciate your feedback and suggestions regarding the FormLayout API, column and row specifications, builder classes and all related issues.
